## Onboarding & Paywall Optimization

### Onboarding Flow

**Goal**: Get users to their first value moment within 30 seconds.

**Recommended flow:**
1. **Welcome screen** (3s) — "Welcome to ArchGee" + two paths shown
2. **Choose your path** — "Find Jobs" or "Try AI Design Tools"
   - Jobs path → Role selection → Location → Start swiping
   - AI Tools path → Show tool gallery → Let user try 1 free generation
3. **No registration required** for first experience

**Anti-patterns to avoid:**
- Don't require sign-up before showing value
- Don't show a paywall before user has engaged meaningfully
- Don't ask for notification permissions on first launch
- Don't show a multi-step tutorial

### Paywall Strategy

**Current model**: Free (4 swipes/day), Premium (unlimited), Credits (AI tools)

**Optimization recommendations:**

1. **Increase free swipes to 10-15/day** — 4 is too restrictive for a niche app. Users need to experience enough value to convert. In a niche with low traffic, every user is precious.

2. **AI tools as the conversion hook** — The AI design tools are a much stronger conversion driver than job swipes because:
   - Results are immediate and visual (dopamine hit)
   - Users want to try multiple styles/rooms (natural desire for "one more")
   - The before/after is shareable (viral potential)

3. **Paywall trigger timing**:
   - **Jobs**: After exhausting daily swipes (soft paywall)
   - **AI Tools**: After 1-2 free generations (hard paywall for credits)
   - Show paywall with the AI result visible but watermarked or at lower resolution

4. **Paywall messaging**:
   - Jobs: "Never miss your dream architecture job — upgrade for unlimited swipes"
   - AI Tools: "Love your redesign? Get unlimited AI generations"
   - Include before/after preview of what premium unlocks

5. **Cross-sell**: When a user hits the job swipe limit, also show the AI tools. When an AI tools user finishes, show relevant jobs ("Looking for interior design jobs?"). This creates two hooks per user.
